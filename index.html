Introduction?

This is not so much a portfolio website, but more of a post-whatever-I-want thing where I document my hobbies, academics and other things, while hopefully learning some web development skills. To be clear, this website is not at all built solely by me. A huge chunk of the code is written by my best friends ChatGPT and DeepSeek. Even then, I've learnt a thing or two like adding "defer" when importing a JavaScript script so it works properly.

Regardless, you will find my opinions on math, records (and recommendations?) of games, shows and music, as well as some photos I took while travelling overseas, or just around Singapore. Nothing is out of the realm of possibility here, really. I hope you have some fun seeing my attempt at being creative.

Also, full disclosure. Not a weeaboo for French (an ouiaboo, if you will), in case that header gave you the impression. There was a piece of media that inspired me to inject some French into my website though. I wonder if you can identify it?
