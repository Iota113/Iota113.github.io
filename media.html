Music Player

Music in my opinion is the most important element in any form of media. All of my favorite games and shows feature exceptional soundtracks. I believe it’s the music that often amplifies their emotional impact. Even for otherwise mediocre stuff, a powerful score can elevate the experience — sometimes even retroactively. I’ve found that I often spend more time listening to the music long after I’ve finished a game or show than I spent actually engaging with it.

Here is a nonexhaustive catalogue of some soundtracks I enjoyed dearly. Oh, and below it is a pretty exhasutive list of the media I consumed.
