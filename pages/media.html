Music

Music is in my opinion the most important aspect in any form of media. All my favourite games and shows have great music, and I think the reason I like them so much is how much the music adds to their emotional impact. Even for otherwise mediocre stuff, music can retroactively make them feel more enjoyable because I spent more time listening to their music after I'm done consuming them, than the time spent actually expriencing them.
