History

I started cooking regularly in 2024 after ORD (complete National Service for Singaporeans). I previously learned how to cook Sichuan dishes from my grandma, but never applied myself until then. I do it less frequently now that I am in university, but I still try to cook during holidays.

I primarily do Chinese Sichuan / Hunan dishes (aka spicy stuff) but may delve into other stuff in the future (?)
